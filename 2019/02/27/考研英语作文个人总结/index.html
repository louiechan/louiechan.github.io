考研英语作文个人总结

写在前面

个人总结： 1. 图表作文首段高度概括图表内容，随后详细描述信息 (纵向横向对比，句子连贯，长短交错，关联紧密，多连词，比较词)，再得出结论，提起下文，末段呼应。 2. 小作文，可自定义场景，务求真诚真实。

大作文（图表作文）

开头段句型

the line graph shows/describes/illustrates/reveals/unfolds/compares/summarizes/suggests/outlines ... ## 表示变化的表达 * increase、rise、go up、grow、jump、climb、shoot up、boom * decrease、decline、go down、fall、be reduced、drop、sink、dip、slump、plunge * rapidly、sharply、significantly、dramatically * gradually、moderately、steadily、slowly、slightly * fluctuate between A and B * remain the same、stabilize at、remain constant at、remain steady、stay constant at、maintain the same level、level off/out at ## 表示变化的句型 * there was a steep fall in 1994 followed by a gradual improvement in the subsequent years. * the price rose and fell over the period, but the trend was definitely upwards. * the price plunged dramatically in 1994, but it regained its previous level, before soaring to a new peak. * the number reached its top/peak/bottom at. * the profit doubled from March to May. * the profit increased three times/three-fold from March to May. * its total output value increased by 11.5 percent over its previous year. * the total volume of state purchase in the first quarter rose by 2.5 percent, compared with the same peroid of last year. * a greater number/percentage/proportion of men than women are fund in doing ... * the number/percentage/proportion of men who are employed in managerial position is much/slightly larger than that of women working in the same occupations. * white is about twice as common as red. ## 通用句型 * according to ... * as shown in ... * as can be seen from ... * it can be seen from ... that ... * it is apparent from ... that ... * as portrayed in ...,it is apparent that ... # 小作文(信、通知...) ## 信 ### 开头称呼 * Dear xxx, * Dear Mr. xxx, * Dear Sir or Madam,to Whom It May Concern, 需大写 ### 结尾 * 正式 * Yours faithfully * Yours trully * Yours respectfully * Yours gratefully * Yours cordially * 私人 * Best wishes * Best regards * Best regards * Warmesr regards * Yours ever * Yours cordially ## 通知 ### 格式
